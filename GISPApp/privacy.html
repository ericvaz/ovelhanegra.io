Privacy Policy

GISP Prep

An app by Ovelha Negra · ovelhanegra.io

Last updated: June 27, 2026

The short version

GISP Prep does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your study progress lives only on your device.

Information we collect

None. We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal data. The app works fully offline.

Data stored on your device

Your study activity — answered questions, scores, flashcard progress, bookmarks, and mock-exam results — is saved locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us, and is removed if you delete the app.

Purchases

The optional one-time “Pro” upgrade is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not see or store your payment details. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-party analytics & tracking

The app contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Children's privacy

GISP Prep is a professional study tool intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above.
