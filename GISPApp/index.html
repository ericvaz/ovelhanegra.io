FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is GISP Prep?

GISP Prep is an iOS study app for the GIS Professional (GISP) certification exam. It includes 190+ practice questions with explanations, timed mock exams, flashcards, and performance analytics across all six GIS knowledge domains.

Is it free?

Yes — download and start studying for free. A one-time GISP Prep Pro purchase ($24.99, no subscription) unlocks all mock exams, analytics, the full flashcard library, and Timed/Challenge/Custom practice.

Is it affiliated with GISCI?

No. GISP Prep is an independent study aid and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the GIS Certification Institute (GISCI). All questions are original.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app → tap any Pro feature → the upgrade screen → Restore Purchase. As long as you're using the same Apple Account, Pro restores at no charge.

Does the app collect my data?

No. GISP Prep works fully offline, requires no account, and collects no personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
