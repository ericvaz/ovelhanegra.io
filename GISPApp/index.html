Frequently asked questions

What is GISP Prep?

GISP Prep is a study app for the GIS Professional (GISP) certification. It includes practice questions, flashcards, timed mock exams, and performance analytics across six GIS knowledge domains: Spatial Analysis & Modeling, Cartography & Visualization, GIS Data Structures, Database Design & Management, Remote Sensing, and GIS Professional Practice.

What's included for free, and what is "Pro"?

You can download and start studying for free, including sample questions and a flashcard deck. A one-time Pro purchase unlocks the full question bank, all mock exams, full analytics, weak-area detection, and the complete flashcard library. Pro is a one-time purchase — there is no subscription.

How do I restore my Pro purchase on a new device?

Open the app, tap any Pro feature to bring up the upgrade screen, then tap “Restore Purchase.” As long as you're signed in with the same Apple Account you bought it with, Pro will be restored at no charge.

Is the app affiliated with GISCI or any certification body?

No. GISP Prep is an independent study aid created by Ovelha Negra. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the GIS Certification Institute (GISCI) or any certifying organization. All study content is original.

How do I request a refund?

Purchases are handled by Apple. You can request a refund through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com. If you have any trouble, email us and we'll help.

Does the app track me or collect my data?

No. GISP Prep stores your study progress only on your device and does not collect personal data. See our Privacy Policy for details.
